## Introduction ## 
This project assesses the viability of adding Disconnect Swithches to the Electric Grid of an Electric Cooperative.
It aims to discover how much electric energy can be saved from installing disconnect switches and what case number is the most economical to the business.

## CONCLUSION ##  

Referring from the dashboard presented above, the energy loss drops as more disconnect switches are being added to the system, but how will we know which is more economical? For that, the energy saved per switch visualization can tell us which case has the most energy saved per switch. In conclusion, Case 3 should be the one considered to save the most energy with respect to the ROI for the purchased disconnect switches.
